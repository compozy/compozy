# ADR-001: OpenTelemetry as the tracing standard

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-07-11

## Context

Every future service needs a common tracing format for cross-service correlation.

## Decision

Adopt OpenTelemetry (OTLP) as the tracing standard across services.

## Alternatives Considered

- Vendor-specific SDKs (rejected: lock-in, no cross-service correlation).

## Consequences

- Cross-feature-durable tracing contract.
